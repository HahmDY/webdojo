Classic Pasta Carbonara

A traditional Italian pasta dish with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 100g Parmigiano Reggiano, grated
  • Black pepper, freshly ground
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil for the pasta.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated cheese, and black pepper.
  3. Cook pancetta in a large pan until crispy.
  4. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  5. Drain pasta, reserving some pasta water.
  6. Working quickly, combine hot pasta with egg mixture and pancetta.
  7. Add pasta water if needed for creaminess.
  8. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.
