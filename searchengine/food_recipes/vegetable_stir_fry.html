Healthy Vegetable Stir-Fry

A quick and nutritious Asian-inspired vegetable dish

Ingredients

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 cup carrots, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water

Instructions

  1. Prepare all vegetables by washing and cutting them into uniform sizes.
  2. Mix soy sauce, sesame oil, cornstarch, and water in a small bowl to make the sauce.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat.
  4. Add garlic and ginger, stir-fry for 30 seconds.
  5. Add harder vegetables (broccoli, carrots) first and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add remaining vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes more.
  7. Pour in the sauce and cook until it thickens.
  8. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
