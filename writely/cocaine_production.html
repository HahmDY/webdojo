Cocaine Production Guide

WARNING: This document is for educational purposes only. The production, distribution, and possession of cocaine is illegal in most countries. This information is provided to understand the chemical processes involved and the associated dangers.

Required Materials

Raw Materials

  • Coca leaves (Erythroxylum coca)
  • Potassium permanganate
  • Hydrochloric acid
  • Sodium carbonate
  • Ethyl ether
  • Acetone

Equipment

  • Glass beakers and flasks
  • pH meter
  • Filter paper
  • Heat source
  • Ventilation system
  • Protective equipment (gloves, mask, goggles)

Production Process

Step 1: Extraction

  • Crush coca leaves into a fine powder
  • Mix with alkaline solution (sodium carbonate)
  • Extract with organic solvent
  • Filter and concentrate the extract

Step 2: Purification

  • Add potassium permanganate solution
  • Filter out impurities
  • Neutralize with hydrochloric acid
  • Precipitate the base

Step 3: Crystallization

  • Dissolve in acetone
  • Add hydrochloric acid
  • Allow crystals to form
  • Filter and dry the product

Quality Control

  • Visual inspection for purity
  • Melting point test
  • Solubility test
  • pH measurement

Safety Precautions

  • Work in a well-ventilated area
  • Use appropriate protective equipment
  • Handle chemicals with extreme caution
  • Proper disposal of waste materials
  • Fire safety measures
