Summer Trip to Japan

Overview

This document outlines our planned two-week trip to Japan in August 2023. We'll be visiting Tokyo, Kyoto, and Osaka, experiencing the unique culture, food, and attractions of each city.

Itinerary

Tokyo (Days 1-5)

  • Day 1: Arrival at Narita Airport, check-in at hotel in Shinjuku, evening exploration of the area
  • Day 2: Tsukiji Outer Market, TeamLab Planets, Odaiba
  • Day 3: Asakusa, Senso-ji Temple, Tokyo Skytree
  • Day 4: Shibuya, Harajuku, Meiji Shrine
  • Day 5: Akihabara, Ueno Park, Tokyo National Museum

Kyoto (Days 6-10)

  • Day 6: Shinkansen to Kyoto, check-in at traditional ryokan
  • Day 7: Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kiyomizu-dera Temple
  • Day 8: Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, Monkey Park, Tenryu-ji Temple
  • Day 9: Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), Ryoan-ji Temple
  • Day 10: Free day for exploration and shopping

Osaka (Days 11-14)

  • Day 11: Train to Osaka, check-in at hotel in Dotonbori
  • Day 12: Osaka Castle, Shitennoji Temple
  • Day 13: Universal Studios Japan
  • Day 14: Departure from Kansai International Airport

Accommodations

  • Tokyo: Citadines Central Shinjuku Tokyo
  • Kyoto: Yuzuya Ryokan
  • Osaka: Swissotel Nankai Osaka

Transportation

  • 7-Day Japan Rail Pass for intercity travel
  • IC cards (Suica/Pasmo) for local transportation
  • Airport transfers via Narita Express and Haruka Limited Express

Budget

  • Flights: $1,200 per person
  • Accommodations: $2,000 total
  • Transportation: $500 per person (including JR Pass)
  • Food and activities: $150 per day per person
  • Total estimated budget: $5,000 per person

Packing List

  • Passport and travel documents
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Lightweight clothing for summer weather
  • Portable charger and power adapter
  • Japanese phrasebook or language app
  • Travel insurance
